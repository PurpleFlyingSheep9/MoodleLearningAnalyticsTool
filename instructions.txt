How to use this tool:

1. Click on *Load Data*
(the data is loaded, no visible feedback)

2. Click on *Predict* 
(this button loads the trained model)

3. Choose a student from 
the list of student IDs. (the textbox will 
show the predicted grade for the selected 
student)

4.Click on *How to Improve* 
to get advice on how to improve from your 
predicted grade.
